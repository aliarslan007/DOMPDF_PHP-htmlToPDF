GENERIC Scope of Work

SoluComp to perform EF&I Samsung CDU-30. FSU and VDU are installed at various locations.

Overall, Scope Involved:

  1. Material acceptance from customer.
  2. Providing all the ancillary material to complete the Install and perform power test.

INSTALL:

  1. Install CDU-30, FSU & VDU in existing FIF frame.
  2. Use 19” mounting brackets where needed.
  3. Flush the chassis in cabinets for easy access to SFP’s for Ops team.
  4. Terminate Power “A” and B” to either -48VDC or +24V fuse panel.
  5. Ground the chassis to FIF frame.
  6. Perform power tests.
  7. Install all SFP’s in appropriate slots based on site configuration.
  8. Install RJ45 management cable.
  9. Install required MM or SM fiber based on site configuration.
  10. Label all power cables, grounding cables, and fiber optic cables and take closeup pictures of all the work.
  11. Provide a close out package to Verizon Wireless.
